## How to Update This File

1. When you **start** a room: change `[ ]` to `[~]` in the house diagram + table
2. When you **finish** a room: change `[~]` to `[x]` and add notes
3. When a room is **blocked**: change to `[!]` and add to Blocked Items table
4. When a room **needs human input**: change to `[?]` and STOP
5. Update the progress bar after every change
